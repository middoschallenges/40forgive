Questions

Good to Know

What is 40 Days to Forgiveness?

It's a free, guided practice that helps you work through forgiveness — of others and yourself — over 40 days. Each day you get one short, simple message to read and reflect on.

How long does it take each day?

Just a few minutes. Each message is short and comes with one small, doable step — no long homework or journaling required (unless you want to).

Is it free?

Yes, completely free. Just sign up with your email or join the WhatsApp group to get started.

How does it work?

Once you sign up, you'll start receiving daily messages by email or WhatsApp. Each one includes a short idea and a simple practice for that day. You can go at your own pace.

Do I need to be religious to join?

Not at all. The program draws on Jewish wisdom about forgiveness, but it's built to be simple, warm, and open to anyone looking to let go and find more peace.
