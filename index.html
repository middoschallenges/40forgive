Frequently asked questions

When does it begin?
Rosh Chodesh Elul.
When is the raffle?
After Yom Kippur, iy"H.
Is it okay if I miss a day?
Absolutely. Just continue with the next challenge.
How long does it take?
About one minute each weekday.
Why is this happening during the 40 days between Rosh Chodesh Elul and Yom Kippur?
The 40 days between Rosh Chodesh Elul and Yom Kippur are a powerful time for forgiveness for the Jewish people. When we forgive our fellow Jews, it brings down tremendous blessings from Above. Chazal teach us that when we forgive others, Hashem forgives us, too. (Yoma 87b)

You don’t have to figure it out alone.

Most of us were taught that forgiveness matters — but never how to actually do it. So we carry the weight, wait for it to lift on its own, and quietly wonder why it never does.

This challenge is different. Instead of simply telling you to forgive, it walks beside you through forty small, practical steps — each one gentle enough to do today, and meaningful enough to change how tomorrow feels.
